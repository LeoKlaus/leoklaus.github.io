Hunt Showdown Graphic Settings Analysis (I'm NOT a professional)

All screenshots were taken at 2560*1440 running on an RTX 3070FE paired with a Ryzen 3700X (both stock) and 32Gb of DDR4@3600MT/s

The reference image is always the same, taken at all settings high, SMAA 2TX, tessellation on.

The image on the left is always the one with the higher setting of the two available.

All performance metrics are specific to my system ONLY. They can be used as a point of reference when tuning graphics settings, but the performance impact on your system may vary greatly. I have NOT checked for system bottlenecks, the FPS limiter was set to 144 for all settings.

All images are uploaded in their original quality. Depending on your internet connection, it might take a while to load the images.

The Javascript code for image comparison was taken from W3Schools
